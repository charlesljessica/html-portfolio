The Best Movies (According to Jessica)

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Coco

"CoCo" emotionally draws me into its vibrant and heartfelt world, exploring themes of family, tradition, and the power of music. The film's stunning animation, rich cultural representatiion, and memorable music make it a cherished and captivating journey.

Hocus Pocus

"Hocus Pocus" is a delightful blend of humor and nostalgia, weaving a spell of laughter with its witty dialogue and whimsical characters. The film's enchanting mix of Halloween magic and timeless comedy creates a captivating experience that continues to charm with each rewatch.

The Greatest Showman

"The Greatest Showman" mesmerizes with its spectacular musical performances, celebrating the extraordinary life of P.T. Barnum. The film's infectious energy, powerful songs, and Hugh Jackman's charismatic performance make it a cinematic spectacle that leaves me inspired and entertained.
